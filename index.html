Quality

In response to the rising demand for high quality and problem-free leather goods, we carry out a set of well planned quality control tests for all our items. Our extensive quality assessment procedure ensures that clients receive leather products that are fully evaluated. Very minute attention is paid to inspect our products. The strength, colour & light fastness, water spotting, colour transfer, blistering of finishes, leather jackets / garments, finish adhesion etc., are evaluated and quality approved during different stages of production & finishing. We also pay good attention to accessories used such as zippers, interlinings, buttons etc.
Our comprehensive product inspection & quality controlling programs are based on statistical analysis of finished goods. We make sure that conformance with pre-approved samples is attained. The finished goods are randomly selected for inspection, prior to packaging and shipment. The sole aim of our quality department is to approve the right quality, resulting in decreasing complains / returns and increasing customer loyalty.
